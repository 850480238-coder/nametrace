Frequently Asked Questions

Is this name tracing generator really free? ▾

Yes, completely free with no sign-up required. You can generate and print unlimited worksheets at no cost. There are no watermarks on the printed pages.

What age is name tracing for? ▾

Name tracing is ideal for children aged 3–6 years, typically in preschool and kindergarten. It helps develop fine motor skills and letter recognition at a crucial stage of development.

Can I make tracing worksheets for full sentences? ▾

You can enter multiple words, one per line, and each will get its own practice section. For very long sentences, use Small letter size so all words fit on one page.

What paper size should I use? ▾

US Letter (8.5″ × 11″) is the standard for American classrooms and home printers. If you're outside the US, switch to A4 in the settings panel. Both sizes are supported.

Why does my child need guide lines? ▾

The three-line guide (top, mid, and baseline) teaches correct letter proportions — tall letters reach the top line, short letters stay between mid and base. This builds the muscle memory children need for consistent handwriting.

Do you store the names I type? ▾

No. Everything happens entirely in your browser. Names and words you type are never sent to any server and are not stored anywhere. The worksheet is generated locally on your device.
